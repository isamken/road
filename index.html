RoadLINX employs GPS technology to optimize routing and ensure timely deliveries. For businesses that need to ship temperature-sensitive goods, RoadLINX offers refrigerated trucks, commonly known as "reefer" trucks. Monitoring Systems

RoadLINX's reefer trucks are fitted with advanced monitoring systems that continuously track the internal temperature of the cargo area. This technology alerts drivers and logistics managers to any deviations from the set temperature, allowing for immediate corrective actions to prevent spoilage or damage.

RoadLINX uses intelligent routing software that analyzes various factors, including shipment size, delivery location, and customer preferences, to optimize delivery routes. Inventory Management Systems

To streamline operations, RoadLINX employs sophisticated inventory management systems that track shipments from origin to destination.
